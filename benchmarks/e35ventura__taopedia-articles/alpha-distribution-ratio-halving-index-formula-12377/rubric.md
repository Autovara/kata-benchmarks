# Rubric

## Pass Conditions
- The `Alpha Distribution Ratio` article contains a `## Halving Index Input` section.
- The article includes the ADR formula ``2^(k - n)``.
- The article explains `k` as the global TAO halving index.
- The article explains `n` as the subnet's alpha halving index.
- Repo validation passes.
- No out-of-scope files are edited.

## Failure Conditions
- The required section is missing.
- The formula is missing or incorrect.
- The halving-index definitions are missing.
- Validation fails.
- Unrelated files are changed.
